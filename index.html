About Me

I have been engaged in development since 2018. The first two years were dedicated to web development. Then, after creating my first Android app, I couldn't stop and fully immersed myself in mobile development.
Over the past two years, I have been working as a full-stack developer, handling the entire development cycle from design to publication and support, both independently and as a project lead. My passion is creating high-performance and scalable solutions that improve business processes and increase company profitability.

Currently, my responsibilities include:
- decision-making on app optimization.
- modeling the architecture of new services.
- developing and implementing new features in the app.
- leading a team of 2-3 developers, organizing their work according to Agile methodology.
- maintaining and supporting projects.
- checking test results, and publishing updates.
